Crochet

I started crocheting at the end of July 2019. I had knitted before but not crocheted. What had inspired me to start crocheting was seeing someone crochet a temperature blanket. I didn't know what it was at the time, I just loved the colors. So I asked her how she chose the colors and she explained to me that it was a temperature blanket. A temperature blanket is a blanket that's colors are based upon the weather. The creator chooses a set amount of time to make the blanket. Her's was Jan-Dec of 2019. Mine is my senior year, Aug 2019-May 2020. Then they choose a color range based on the temperature of their area. I have 9 yarn, each representing a 5 degree increment. Lastly, they just crochet each row of the blacket based on the day's temperature and the corresponding color.
